Today's question

Where should the city invest its budget?

The situation

Your city has a limited budget to invest in one public infrastructure improvement. You'll be asked to share which option you'd choose and why — your input will be considered alongside everyone else's in this room.

Building a new bike lane
Renovating a local public park
Installing water fountains across the city

How today's session works

Phase 1

Share your view

You'll have a private conversation with an AI moderator. It will ask you which option you'd choose and why. Your response is confidential.

Between phases

Ideas are collected

Once everyone has responded, the reasoning from all participants is anonymised and neutrally rephrased — no names, no attribution.

Phase 2

Hear others' thinking

You'll return to the chat to read what others in the room said — and reflect on whether it changes or reinforces your view.

Outcome

Collective picture

We'll see how views shifted (or didn't) after exposure to each other's reasoning, without anyone having to argue directly.
